About

Bio

Building, performing, writing; science, art and all of it applied to practice: It would be unfair to all my other interests to choose only to highlight one.

As a builder and maker in my high school's Robotics club, I love drafting designs for our robots according to their tasks and needs and collaborating with the coders to make sure our robots perform to the highest degrees possible. As the first female builder member also, I hope make this scene more egalitarian by helping create a more welcoming space so future robotics experts can also start their journeys early and gladly.

I am also a musician: playing the drums and the piano for various genres including classic, jazz and rock, getting to go on stage to perform and teaching others music to find their own potentials all help me nourish my creativity and zeal.

Last but not least, I am a writer. Prose and sometimes poetry are great outlets for my imagination and an excellent way for me to share both my feelings with the world and my knowledge with my peers.

I see one crucial thread connecting all these disparate interests and skills: Creation. The desire to create and share compels me in all these areas, which is why I am excited to jump into the world of higher education.
